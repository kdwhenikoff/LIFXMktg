What LIFX Is

LIFX is a wifi-enabled, energy efficient, "normal" and multi-colored bulb you control with your iPhone or Android. It gives you unprecedented control of your lights, reduces your energy costs, lasts up to 25 years and delivers an amazing range of experiences we think you’ll love. Simply replace your existing bulbs with LIFX smartbulbs, download and install our free app from the App Store or Google Play and you're good to go.

LIFX Vision

LIFX answers a need that is truly overdue. Everyone in the world needs light bulbs but, existing bulbs consume approximately 20 percent of electricity in homes around the world. Bulbs need to be more efficient, smarter, and last longer. Also, "home automation" and having a “smart home” intrigue many, but remain unattainable goals for most people. This understanding has been one of the guiding forces in the design of the LIFX smartbulb — to start with one commonly used household item and make it smart, easy to use and accessible to everyone.
Phil Bosua, Inventor of the LIFX smartbulb

How LIFX Works

  • Screw in the smartbulb and launch the free LIFX app.
  • The LIFX "master" bulb auto configures to your router.
  • Any additional "slave" bulbs auto connect to the master, using a mesh network.
  • Each smartbulb can be controlled individually, and you can use any light switch to turn your smartbulbs on and off.
  • Will approximate a 60-watt incandescent bulb or a 50-watt halogen downlight.
